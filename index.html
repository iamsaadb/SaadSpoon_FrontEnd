Fine Dining

SaadSpoon is a modern interpretation of a cuisine deeply rooted in the past. Our food pays homage to those roots, showcasing the rich and vibrant flavors of Morocco in new ways that harmonize with the fresh, local, artisanal ingredients, and the spirit of innovation of Northern California. Located at 260 Polk Street, in the newly renovated Union Building, SaadSpoon graciously features authentic Moroccan design in modern interpretation, being in perfect harmony with the cuisine.

Our Legacy

Moroccan cooking is very popular in the Western world, especially Europe. It usually ranks in the five most popular ethnic cuisines in countries such as France, Belgium, the U.K. and Germany. However, in the U.S., Moroccan cuisine is not yet familiar to many people. And, in large cities such as New York and Chicago, there are only a handful of Moroccan restaurants. A few prepared foods, such as sauces, condiments, herbs and spice mixes are sold in America, and they mostly are found in ethnic and specialty stores.Although Moroccan cooking is influenced by many culture and cuisines like Jewish, French and Spanish cuisines, it is not as popular as we think it should be. SaadSpoon is making efforts everyday, to satisfy more customers. And this is why, we wanted to preserve our legacy and showcasing it in the Bay Area. Discovering new market is good, but sharing and introducing our food and culture is absolutely something we cherish and we thrive for!
